What We Offer

Educational Programs

We offer interactive learning experiences for all ages, from school field trips to adult workshops about wildlife conservation and animal behavior.

Conservation Efforts

Our zoo participates in international breeding programs and supports wildlife conservation projects around the globe.

Visitor Experiences

Enjoy guided tours, animal feeding sessions, and special events throughout the year. Our hotel offers the unique experience of staying overnight near your favorite animals.

Our Mission

At Riget's Zoo, our mission is to inspire conservation of wildlife through education, research, and providing exceptional care for our animal family. We strive to create meaningful connections between humans and animals while promoting environmental stewardship.

Founded in 1975, we have grown from a small local attraction to a world-class facility that houses over 300 species and welcomes more than 500,000 visitors annually. Our commitment to animal welfare, conservation, and education continues to guide every aspect of our operations.
